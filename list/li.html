Paneer do payaza:)

Ingredients:-

  • 200 grams pnaeer or indian cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup onions finely chopped
  • 1/3 tomatoes chopped (1 large . or replace with curd)
  • 1/3 cupcapsicum cubed
  • 7 to 8 cashews
  • 2 to 3 tbsps oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin or jeera
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1 1/4 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 3/4 tsp garam mashala
  • 3/4 coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt (use as needed)
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 dried fenugreek leaves
  • 1 1/2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 12 shallots - small onions or 1/4 heaped cup cubed
  • 2 green chillies deseeded and sliced

Preparation for paneer do payaza:-

  1. make a fine puree of capsicum,tomatoes and cashews.Set the aside
  2. Add oilto a pan and heat it
  3. Add jeera and cardamoms
  4. when they splutter, add chopped onions
  5. fry until they turn golden
  6. Add ginger garlic paste and fry until theraw small of ginger garlic go away
  7. pour the tomato puree and sauteon a low to medium flame
  8. within minutes the mixture begins to leave the pan
  9. Add salt , turmeric,red chilli powder,garam masala and corinander powder
  10. saute till oil separates .After frying the mixture for few minutes,oil begins to oozeout
  11. pour water and mix well
  12. cook covered until the gravy thickens.Traces of oil would be seen on the gravy
  13. lower the flame completely.add paneer and sprinkle crushed kasuri methi 😀
  14. cook covered just for 2 to 3 mins.turn off the stove. do not over cook the paneer 😀
  15. meanwhile add 1 to 1.5 tbsp oil or ghee to a hot pan. fry onions and green chillies on high flame just for 2 to3 mins.the onions must be partially cooked but must retain the crunch 😀
  16. pour this over the paneer gravy and sprinkle some coriander leaves.you can set aside few onions for garnish 😀
  17. paneer do payaza is ready to serve 😀
